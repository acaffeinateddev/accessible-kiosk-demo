REPRESENTS ADA REACH ZONE LIMIT OF 48"

Accessible Kiosk Demo

This is a demonstration of a potential solution for certain kiosk models where only a small part of the screen falls within the ADA's "reach zone" guidelines of 48" from the ground.

In order to keep the buttons a reasonable size on mobile (for demonstration purposes), the instructional text at the bottom is a bit small! Please pretend it is a reasonable size as it would be on an actual kiosk display.

The instructional text is as follows:

For easier access, use the buttons on the left or right to move the interactive area up or down.

Please make a selection.

For easier access, use the buttons on the left or right to move the interactive area up or down.
